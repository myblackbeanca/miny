Pre-orders

What is a pre-order?

Pre-save an album or playlist on MINYFY before it's released.

How long are pre-orders available?

Pre-orders are available for up to 12 weeks. If you want to release your music in less than 12 weeks, you can use Rapid Connect to get your music on MINYFY faster.

Can I edit the release date of a pre-order?

You can't edit the release date of a pre-order after it's been submitted. To change the release date, you need to remove the pre-order from the platform and resubmit it as a new release.

Shipping

What is Rapid Connect?

Rapid Connect allows you to distribute your music to MINYFY within 24-48 hours instead of the standard 2-3 weeks.

Who can use Rapid Connect?

Rapid Connect is available to all artists and labels at no additional cost.

Can I use Rapid Connect to update an existing release?

No. Rapid Connect is only available for new releases. You cannot use Rapid Connect to update existing releases.
